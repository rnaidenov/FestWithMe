Junction 2 merges natural beauty, green fields, rambling rivers, woodland pathways and hidden enclaves - all dominated by striking industrial features that will make the site iconic.<br />
<br />
The message is simple - great sound, high-end production, an obsessive attention to detail, no short sets, no VIP, no plastic tents, like-minded people and a solid electronic music policy. <br />
<br />
For 2018, Junction 2 have put a firm focus on the music, making space for artists to indulge in extended sets and enjoy the freedom to showcase their full range of sounds. In addition, festival organisers, LWE, go to pain-staking lengths to provide stand-out sound and production, as well as carefully selected food and drink vendors, at London's most diverse day-festival location.<br />
<br />
Created by LWE, 2018 will mark the third edition of the festival.<br />
