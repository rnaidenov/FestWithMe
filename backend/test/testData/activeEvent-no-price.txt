A whole new musical experience<br />
<br />
Awakenings and Time Warp connect to bring you an unprecedented experience. A synthesis of two brands with a long-standing history, both acclaimed for their distinctive productions, blending into a whole new music experience.<br />
<br />
Music guides our hearts and feet, until they move at the same pace, at the speed of the beat - uniting us in sound and vision.<br />
<br />
For one night on October 13th 2018, Düsseldorf will be the center of the electronic music world and a link between the homes of Awakenings and Time Warp.
</p>
